Acacia

Privacy Policy

Acacia is designed as a local-first PDF workspace. The core app stores your document library, tags, collections, reading progress, comments, annotations, bookmarks, signatures, and compare state locally on your device.

Documents

Acacia does not upload the PDF documents you open, annotate, compare, or export. Your original files remain on your Mac unless you choose to share or export them using macOS actions.

Accounts and Pro Features

If you choose to sign in or use Pro features, Acacia may process account identifiers, purchase receipts, subscription status, entitlement state, and basic diagnostic request metadata needed to provide and validate those features. This data is used to operate the service and is not used for tracking.

Analytics and Advertising

Acacia does not include advertising SDKs or third-party tracking. We do not sell personal information.

Support

If you contact support, we use the information you provide to respond to your request. Email privacy questions to support@benebsworth.com.
